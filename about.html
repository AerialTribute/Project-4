Our Theory

The greatest advances in aviation came by normal people who decided to do something for a cause bigger than themselves. We believe that people who embody selfless motivation are more likely to reach their goals and help us in reshaping the way the public views general aviation.

How We Got Started

The Aerial Tribute Project was born out of a simple but powerful belief: that access to aviation and the skies should not be limited to privilege. Our team consists of volunteers, pilots, and advocates who saw a gap in the industry and decided to do something about it.

Since our founding, we’ve worked with local communities, partnered with responsible flight schools, and funded aspiring pilots who reflect a strong sense of service and grit. We’re on a mission to transform general aviation...one tribute, one flight, one story at a time.
